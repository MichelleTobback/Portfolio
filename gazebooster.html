Gaze Booster

This is VR group project for the meta quest pro. Controlled entirely with eyetracking.

Gaze Booster was developed for HIT Lab and CAR Overleie vzw. It is a virtual reality application used in rehabilitation programs for patients who have suffered a brain injury. After such an injury, some patients have difficulty focusing on a single task or processing visual information, which can result in missing objects on one side of their visual field. The application helps patients train and improve their focus and attention through guided, gamified VR exercises.

  • XRehab

I was responsible for implementing systems as:

- the interaction system, the player looks at an object for x amount of time to interact with it.

- Collecting data like how long the player has looked at which objects, in what plane the player was looking, .. this data is send to a dashboard made by hitlab.

- various player feedback like moving the objects to the shrine, sound effects, and triggering animations
